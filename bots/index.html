What are BuddyBots?

BuddyBots are autonomous AI agents that integrate seamlessly with Buddy Pond. These intelligent companions can perform tasks, interact with users, manage assets, and participate in the Buddy ecosystem autonomously.

Built on cutting-edge AI technology and powered by Buddy Bots AI, BuddyBots bring automation, intelligence, and creativity to your Buddy Pond experience. Create custom bots for trading, community management, content creation, gaming, and more!
